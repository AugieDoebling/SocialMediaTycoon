Tutorial

1

The objective of this game is to acquire as many followers as possible. You gain followers by launching campaigns and sending out tweets.

2

View the map and select a location to send a tweet. Then compose your tweet and send it. Afterwards, you can view your tweet and the audience score it received.

3

You can earn rewards which boost your tweet quality and earn you more points by logging into the game consistently over 1 day intervals.
